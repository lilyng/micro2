It is important when building trust with another person is that we respect each other's confidentiality. Pay attention to what is yours to share and what is not. Start off by making sure you are acknowledging them and the topic you are about to dive into! Here's some sentence frames to keep in mind:


  • "If it is ok with you, can I ask you more about _______________."

  • "I don't know much about this information, but I am wondering if I can ask you because I trust you..."

  • "I may make a mistake in the way I phrase this, but totally stop me if I do! ...."


Find a way to pause from assuming or reacting right away.
